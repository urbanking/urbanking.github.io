Research directions

Three connected research questions.

View all research →
01

Can LLM agents reproduce human travel choices?

I build behaviorally aligned persona agents that extend small paired RP–SP surveys into population-scale experiments for autonomous shuttles and DRT.

Travel behavior
02

How can vehicles and infrastructure reason together?

I study cooperative world models for autonomous driving at signalized intersections, linking physical context with decision-making.

Physical AI
03

How do we keep mobility AI grounded?

I combine behavioral constraints, retrieval, held-out human responses, and reproducible evaluation to test where AI systems succeed—and fail.

Trustworthy AI
